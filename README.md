The upgrades so far are as follows:

Health
- Tier 1: Gain +20 additional health. [DONE]
- Tier 2: Reduce all incoming damage by 5%. [DONE]
- Tier 3: Gain an adaptive regeneration abality.
    + If you are below 50% health, gain +0.5 hp/s regen until back up to 50%.
    +  If you are below 100% but above 50% health and have not taken damage again 25 seconds after the first time, gain a passive + 2 hp/s buff until back up to 100%. Can be stopped if taking damage again.

Stamina
    - Tier 1: Decrease running stamina usage by 30%. [DONE] [NOT SATISFACTORY]
    - Tier 2: Improve stamina regen by 10%.
    - Tier 3: Reduced stamina regen and speed penalty when dealing with heavier inventories (35lbs or higher). 

Movement
    - Tier 1: Sprint 6% faster. [DONE]
    - Tier 2: Walk/Crouch 12% faster. [DONE]
    - Tier 3: Increased jump height by 25%. [DONE]

Utilities
    - Tier 1: Increase all battery capacities by 10%.
    - Tier 2: Reduce cost of all store items by 5%. 
    - Tier 3: Flashlight items can pass through the inverse teleporter.

Legendaries (Acquired after buying the respective Tier 3 upgrade.)
    - Health: Additional +20 health. 
    - Stamina: When in danger, gain infinite stamina. After out of danger, ability goes on cooldown for 30 seconds.
    - Movement: Gain dead silence for 10 seconds on activation. Goes on cooldown for 40 seconds.
    - Utilities: All equipment weighs 0 pounds.
